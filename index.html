My Lotus Journey

Hey Sakshi,

I know you know me. I'm Rushikesh. You don't like me even a little bit. You have so much hatred for me, but it's okay. Good things take time.

I don't even know why I like you so much. I don't know myself, but it's fine. Let it be. To be honest, I've been waiting for you for 2 years.

You'll unblock me now, you'll do it now, but you didn't. I kept waiting. But like a lotus, I rose from the mud. Now I'm stronger.

I learned from your hatred, I grew from your silence. Now I've found myself. No matter how you are, I'm on my journey now.

Not for you, but for myself, I'm blooming. If you ever understand my value, my door will be open. Otherwise, I'll keep walking on my lotus path.

- Rushikesh, who bloomed even from mud 🌸
